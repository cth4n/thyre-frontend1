So, we're finally doing this.

Thyre is a US-based development studio, we focus on developing solutions for companies and developers. We started with a simple goal: build tools and products that developers actually want to use, and build them properly.

Our first product

Our first product, Aviaramp, is a fully animated PBR aircraft boarding stair system built for Roblox developers. It reflects the kind of quality and attention to detail you can expect from everything we ship.

What's next

We're not stopping at aviation. In Q2, we're planning to release products that better suit a wider range of genres and use cases. We have a lot in the pipeline and we're looking forward to sharing more as we get closer.

Getting involved

If you're interested in keeping up with what we're building, follow us on our socials and keep an eye on this blog. We'll be posting updates, product releases and dev notes here as things progress.

We appreciate the support, and we're just getting started.
